Alcalá de Henares

The 5 most famous sweets of Alcala de Henares

Alcala de Henares is well known for its cuisine, but fame and quality of its sweets also attracts lots of tourists and lovers of pastries. Among our most popular products include the costrada , the almonds Alcalá or pralines, the Shortbread de Alcalá , the Texas and Penitentes , so popular during the Easter Alcalá .
