Gastronomy in Alcala de Henares

Gastronomy of Alcala de Henares is one of the most comprehensive, rich and varied of the Community of Madrid. A wealth rooted in the Spain of the Golden Age and the kitchen is referenced propagated by Miguel de Cervantes in Don Quixote. There is no better place to enjoy an evocative and unique cuisine like Complutense streets, full of history and places that have deserved to be recognized as a World Heritage Site.

The 5 most famous sweets of Alcala de Henares

Alcala de Henares is well known for its cuisine, but fame and quality of its sweets also attracts lots of tourists and lovers of pastries. Among our most popular products include the costrada , the almonds Alcalá or pralines, the Shortbread de Alcalá , the Texas and Penitentes , so popular during the Easter Alcalá .
