Route of Cervantes

Route of Cervantes

The itinerary covers places of Alcala de Henares related to the life and work of Miguel de Cervantes: The house where he was born in 1547, the buildings related to his family or those linked to the publication of their works.


  • Interpretation Center Universes of Cervantes
  • College of Theologians of the Mother of God
  • Plaza and Statue of Cervantes
  • Mayor Colegio de San Ildefonso
  • Printing of La Galatea
  • Museo Casa Natal de Cervantes
  • Hospital de Antezana
  • House Calzonera
  • Convento from image

We started in the old hostelry of Students , XVI-XVII centuries, next to the Municipal Tourist Office Plaza de Cervantes.
