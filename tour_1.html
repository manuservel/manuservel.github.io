The city of three cultures

The city of three cultures

The medieval Alcalá, with its Christian neighborhoods, Jewish and Muslim, traveled through a route that is marking the places of these historic areas. And the memory space of the mosque and the ancient synagogues, the slaughter of the Jews, merchants, churches, offices and places that shaped and character to a medieval village full of contrasts and an example of coexistence.


We started at the Interpretation Centre Burgo de Santiuste, next to the walled enclosure.


TRAVEL


The plates, made of ceramic, are located in places protagonists of medieval Alcalá. According to the district to which they relate, in them a cross, a crescent or seven-branched candelabrum it appears as a symbol. In each area a general plate where its boundaries and location of the remaining signals are marked stands.
