Where You Can Find Us


Do you want to enjoy a new tourist experience? In Alcalá de Henares you have different guided visits to see their heritage and live their streets. Join us!

We offer guided tours with routes that reflect the diverse cultures which have come through Alcalá over more than twenty centuries.

  • The "City of Three Cultures" route takes you back to the medieval Alcalá of Christian, Jewish and Muslim neighbourhoods through the historic city centre.
  • The "Cervantes" route. The itinerary covers places related to the life and work of Miguel de Cervantes.
  • The "Literary City" tour goes beyond Miguel de Cervantes to take you to places related to the work of writers from other eras who lived, studied and wrote in Alcalá. Arcipreste de Hita, Fray Luis de León, Francisco de Quevedo...
  • The "Nocturnal visit Alcalá Magic" route. Magic, witchcraft, the Inquisition, the medieval prison and other ancient legends.
  • The "Free Tour". A visit that will bring us to those fundamental places in the history of Alcala de Henares.
